Intro:

The C.I.S.R is a communist organization that began to organize between 2016 and 2019 in Algeria. We are heirs to the history of the Italian communist left in Algeria, known as "Bordigist." We stand in continuity with the journal El Oumami, an Algerian political journal from the 1980s.

This website aims to present the C.I.S.R cell in Lebanon, where for more than two and a half years now, the C.I.S.R has been engaged in the anti-imperialist and anti-colonialist struggle waged by the working classes in Lebanon, occupied Palestine, Syria, Yemen, Iraq, and now Iran, against the aggression of the Zionist entity — found guilty of genocide — which is waging a total imperialist and colonialist war against all working classes of the region, with the stated objective of carrying out a large-scale ethnic cleansing project.

Our website aims to share our political analyses, but above all to provide the theoretical material and political directives enabling the formation and organization of revolutionary combat vanguards throughout the Arab world, from the Middle East to the Maghreb.

Our objective is clear: **we seek to position ourselves within the historical window that allows the transformation of the struggle of transnational resistance, within the framework of this regional war, into an international revolutionary struggle.**
